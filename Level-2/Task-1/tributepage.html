Mr. Rohit Gurunath Sharma

Hitman of India

About

Rohit Sharma, known as the "Hitman" of Indian cricket, is one of the most explosive and elegant opening batsmen in the world. He holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs (264 runs) and has multiple centuries in all formats. He also won t20 world cup as capitan in 2024 and champions trophy in 2025

Qualities

  • Calm Under Pressure
  • Explosive Hitter
  • Cricketing Intelligence
  • Mentor & Role Model
  • Consistency
  • Record Breaker

Legacy

Rohit Sharma redefined opening batting with his elegance and explosive power. He holds records like the highest ODI score (264) and three double centuries. As a leader, he guided India and Mumbai Indians to multiple major titles. His calmness, consistency, and inspiration make him a true cricketing legend.
